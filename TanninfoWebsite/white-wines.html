Now back to the main question: What white wines are for you? Our goal is to find you the best wines for you and the best wines in your area. One of the big differences between reds and whites is the taste: White wines typically have a dry, crisp, fruity flavor. While red wines tend to have rich and bitter taste. So it's up to you decide which one you may prefer. Our job here is to help you determine which you may prefer based on a flavor profile. From there you will be able to learn which wines are considered the best based on our database and learn about the different foods you will be able to pair with your new found wines.

Gosset Champagne By far our top white wine suggestion. The top selling wine in Decatur, this is a very crisp wine that pairs well with scallops and oysters.

Bouvet Rose

Our second white wine suggestion. The Bouvet Rose is very dry and crisp in taste, but pairs well with a light pasta and rice dishes, especially with seafood such as shellfish and fish.

Laporte Sauv Blanc

A medium bodied wine. The Laporte Sauv Blancs tartiness lightens any dish while enhancing the flavors at the same time. This wine pairs best with vegetable-heavy dishes.

Vietti Moscato

A fruity, light-bodied to medium bodied wine, the Vietti Moscato pairs best with spicy dishes such as Thai or Indian Curry.
